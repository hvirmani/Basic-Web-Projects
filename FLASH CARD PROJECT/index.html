Goals Give You Focus Without a goal, your efforts can become disjointed and often confusing.Being able to measure progress is extremely rewarding and will help you maintain focus, keep your head held high and your energy up. It will also keep you from getting down.

Dharma

Dharma is the power that maintains society, it makes the grass grow, the sun shine, and makes us moral people or rather gives humans the opportunity to act virtuously.Dharma is known to be the everlasting directing force which has directed the original human passage on Earth.

Karma

Result of a person's actions as well as the actions themselves

Karma is the intentions you had, behind all your past actions and current ones. Example: You may have did something good for someone in the past, with the intention in your mind that from that action, that person will now owe you something in the future that you can collect off of.

Role

Role is the act of imitating the character and behavior

Being responsible means doing things which are not only beneficial to us but also to the society. Responsibilities can't be taught, it's something that we learn and decide by ourselves. Our ethics play a major role. Every person has a different set of rules based on which they prioritize their actions.
